Services We Can Offer

Yoga

Yoga focuses on three major factors in fitness; our mind, body, and soul. Yoga helps increase our body's strength, core stability & flexibility whilst helping improve our heart & circulatory health. At Zest Fitness Studio we focus on each individual's needs to help increase your body both physically and mentally. We look at all aspects of Yoga for clients to ensure we improve each and every one that enters our studio doors.

Treadmill

Treadmills provide outstanding cardiovascular exercises, which can significantly enhance your heart health. Thanks to their ability to maintain constant heart rates throughout the exercise. If you've high cholesterol or cardiovascular issues, this can be very beneficial.

Weghtlefting

Weight training is also known as resistance or strength training. It involves moving parts of your body against some kind of resistance, like weights, resistance bands, weight machines, or even your own body weight. Weight training is an excellent way to build muscle mass and make your muscles stronger.

Boxing

Boxing is one of the best forms of cardio in addition to improving hand and eye coordination and decreasing stress. Boxing can be done by all age groups and offers a form of self-defense. At Zest Fitness Studio, you can practice in a boxing ring for a great experience while working out.
